Welcome to HydroCharge ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application HydroCharge (the "App").

Our App is a fitness utility designed to help users track their daily water intake and stay hydrated. We prioritize your privacy and do not collect unnecessary data.

3. Information We Collect

Information We DO NOT Collect

HydroCharge is designed to function as a local tool. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). Specifically:

  • No Personal Data: We do not collect your name, physical address, phone number, or biometric data.
  • No Account Creation: You can use all features of the app without creating an account or providing an email.
  • No Payment Data: The app does not process payments or store financial information.
  • No Precise Location: We do not track your GPS or movements.

Information We DO Collect

To provide a better user experience, we may collect minimal non-identifying technical data:

  • Usage Statistics: Anonymous data on how often buttons are clicked to improve the UI.
  • Device Logs: Basic information like device model and OS version to fix crashes and ensure compatibility.

💧 Data Storage: All hydration logs (amount of water consumed) are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

4. How We Use Your Information

Any minimal data collected is used solely for:

  • Maintaining the technical stability of the App.
  • Improving the "HydroCharge" experience and animation performance.
  • Analyzing aggregate trends to optimize features for our users.

5. Third-Party Services

HydroCharge does not share your data with third-party advertisers or data brokers. We do not use social media tracking pixels.

6. Children's Privacy

HydroCharge is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App promotes a healthy lifestyle through hydration tracking.

7. Data Retention

Since we do not maintain user profiles, no personal data is retained on our side. Your hydration history stays on your phone until you clear the App data or uninstall it.

8. Your Rights

You have the full right to use the App privately. You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the App. For any inquiries regarding your data, please contact us at dobudahu387@gmail.com.
